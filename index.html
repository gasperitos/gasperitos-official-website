About Gasperitos

I'm a solo indie developer — I handle the code, the pixel art, and the game design myself, end to end. My focus is small, focused games with systems that reward experimentation.

I build in the open: new versions ship as public devlogs that rework mechanics, retune balance and fix issues based directly on what players run into. My main project, Darkeon, has reached thousands of players on PC and Android.
